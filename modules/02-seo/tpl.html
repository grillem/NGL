NextGenLab

The NextGen Lab is a global platform that connects people, organizations, and ideas to drive a more sustainable future. It acts as a catalyst for change, building innovation ecosystems through thematic clusters, collaborative events, and knowledge networks. Its mission is to restore the relationship between humanity and nature, empower the next generation of leaders, and transform how we collaborate for the planet's wellbeing. Based in Barcelona with an international outlook, The NextGen Lab promotes initiatives that blend sustainability, technology, education, and human potential development.
